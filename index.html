How It Works

Three steps. Zero friction.
Total protection.

Step 01

Prompt Interception

GuardLogic deploys as a lightweight proxy or browser extension. Every prompt typed into any AI tool — ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude, Gemini — is silently captured before transmission.

1
2
Step 02

AI-Powered Detection

A fine-tuned detection model scans for PII, credentials, financial data, IP, and contextually sensitive content — not just pattern-matching, but semantic understanding.

Step 03

Real-Time Protection

Sensitive tokens are masked, tokenized, or blocked in under 1ms. The cleaned prompt is forwarded to the AI tool. Your team sees a normal response. Your data never leaves.

PII Masking

Your data goes in.
Secrets stay out.

GuardLogic replaces sensitive values with meaningless tokens — so your team still gets a useful AI response, but the AI model never sees the real data.

❌ Before GuardLogic

CEO of Nexus Dynamics Inc.
Her email: sarah@nexus.io
DOB: 14/03/1982
Contract value: $2.4M
✓ After GuardLogic
Draft an NDA for [PERSON_A],
CEO of [COMPANY_X].
Her email: [EMAIL_f4a2]
DOB: [DOB_e9c1]
Contract value: [AMOUNT_b3d7]

🔢 Tokenization

Real values are swapped for random tokens like [EMAIL_f4a2]. The AI sees structure without seeing data. Tokens are reversible when you need the real output.

🧠 Context-Aware Masking

GuardLogic doesn't just look for patterns. It understands context — so it catches "our Q3 revenue" or an implied SSN just as well as a formatted phone number.

🔐 Reversible Encryption

For advanced workflows, GuardLogic encrypts PII values using a private key only you control. Responses from the AI can be decrypted post-response — keeping data useful and safe.
